## 📥 Export Settings (For All Videos)

**Recommended Export Settings:**

- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 (vertical for TikTok/Reels) or 1080x1920
- **Frame Rate:** 60fps (for smooth motion)
- **Bitrate:** 8000–12000 Kbps (for good quality)
- **Audio:** AAC, 192 Kbps, 44.1kHz
- **Duration:** Match your script length
- **Text Overlays:** PNG format for sharp text

## 💡 Pro Tips

**For Best Results:**

1. **Be Specific:** Describe camera angles, lighting, colors
2. **Mention Duration:** Add "60fps" and desired length
3. **Include Style:** "cinematic", "realistic", "professional"
4. **Describe Transitions:** Use "fade", "wipe", "cut" instructions
5. **Add Text Style:** "text overlay", "bold white letters", "centered"
6. **Include Branding:** Add Focusmate logo and URL

Privacy, access, correction, deletion, and other data requests may be sent to stawelba@gmail.com.

Scope

This policy applies to procrastination-fix.com and information you submit through forms linked from the website. The website is intended for people aged 18 and older. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

Information We Collect

  • Form information: Your email address, an optional name, and an optional response describing what you would like help with.
  • Communications: Information contained in messages you send to us and our responses.
  • Technical information: Cloudflare may process IP addresses, browser and device information, request details, and security logs when delivering and protecting the website.

How and Why We Use Information

  • Responding to requests: To answer your message, provide requested content, and suggest resources relevant to the help you requested.
  • Affiliate promotions: To send relevant recommendations and promotional communications where you have agreed to receive them. You may opt out at any time.
  • Website delivery and security: To operate, protect, troubleshoot, and prevent misuse of the website.
  • Legal compliance: To maintain necessary records and respond to lawful requests.

Depending on the law that applies to you, these activities are based on your consent, steps taken at your request, our legitimate interest in responding and operating a secure website, or compliance with a legal obligation. Where we rely on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting earlier lawful processing.

Procrastination-fix participates or intends to participate in affiliate programmes operated by Focusmate, ClickUp, and Todoist. We may earn a commission when you follow an affiliate link and complete a qualifying action, at no additional cost to you.

We do not give your Google Form responses or email address to these affiliate partners for commission purposes. When you leave our website, the partner independently controls the information it collects through its website, account, cookies, and transaction process.

Service Providers and Disclosures

We do not sell your submitted personal information. We do not disclose it to third parties for their own direct marketing unless you separately authorize that disclosure.

International Processing

Google, Cloudflare, and affiliate partners may process information in countries other than Ghana or your country of residence. Those countries may have different privacy laws. Where required, we rely on the safeguards offered by the relevant provider and any legally recognized transfer mechanism.

Technical information processed by service providers is retained according to their applicable settings and policies.

Security

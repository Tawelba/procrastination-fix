## 🎬 STEP 3: Generate & Export (1–3 minutes)

### RunwayML Generation:
1. Go to RunwayML → Gen-2 (text-to-video)
2. Paste your prompt
3. Set duration to match script
4. Click "Generate"
5. Wait 1–3 minutes
6. Preview and download MP4

### Pika Labs Generation:
1. Go to Pika Labs → "Generate" tab
2. Paste your prompt
3. Choose model: Pika v1.5
4. Click "Generate"
5. Wait 30 seconds–2 minutes
6. Download MP4

## 💡 Pro Tips to Get Better Results

### For RunwayML:
- 🎨 Use "Style" dropdown: "Cinematic", "Realistic"
- 🎬 Add camera movement: "Camera slowly pans around room"
- 💡 Add time of day: "Daylight window lighting"
- ✨ Add lighting: "Soft shadows", "Warm colors"

### For Pika Labs:
- 🎯 Use "Aspect Ratio": "9:16 vertical"
- 🎬 Choose "Motion Scale": 0.6–0.8 (not too chaotic)
- 💡 Add "Text Overlay" if supported

### For Voiceovers:
- 🗣️ Use "Adam" voice on ElevenLabs (most professional)
- 🎯 Adjust speed to 0.95x for natural pacing
- 🎭 Choose "Narration" style in settings
